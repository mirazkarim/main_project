About us

New income

First you need to mention the name of where your income came from in the new income field. Then you need to write down the amount of money coming from the income. After writing, if you click on new income, the new income will be deposited automatically.

All income

Then all the types of income we were adding weekly, monthly and yearly will be sequentially arranged in a chapter called All Income. So that we can know our sources of income very easily.

New Expenses

You have to go to the new expense and mention the name from where you have incurred the expense. Then write the amount of money spent below. After writing, if you click on the new cost, the new cost will be accumulated automatically. Besides that, the balance of the new expenses will be deducted from the current balance.

All Expenses

Then the weekly, monthly and yearly expenses that we were adding all the expenses will be organized sequentially in a chapter called All Expenses. So that we can easily know the sources of our expenses. Apart from that, we can see the list of how much money we are spending in a month and we can get ideas from the expenses section on how to reduce the expenses in the next month.
